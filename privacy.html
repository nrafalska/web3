Calgary Funds – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Calgary Funds (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you visit calgaryfunds.com or use any services under the Calgary Funds brand.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: name, email address, phone number, billing address.
  • Account Credentials: username, password, profile details.
  • Usage & Analytics: pages viewed, session duration, click-streams.
  • Device & Diagnostics: IP address, browser type, OS, crash logs.
  • Transactional Data: purchase history, payment method info, invoices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain and improve the Website and Services.
  • Authenticate access to protected areas.
  • Process payments, refunds and send invoices.
  • Personalize your experience and recommend content.
  • Communicate updates, support messages and marketing.
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: payment processors, hosting, analytics.
  • Business Transfers: mergers, acquisitions, asset sales.
  • Legal Requests: to comply with laws or protect our rights.
  • Affiliates: entities under common control.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, pixels and similar tools to:

  • Remember preferences and login status.
  • Measure performance and site usage.
  • Enable social media integrations.

You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may limit functionality.
