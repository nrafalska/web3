About NY Capital Investments

At NY Capital, we’ve reimagined investing with technology, data science, and personalized guidance.

Company Overview

Since 2018, NY Capital has disrupted traditional wealth management by offering algorithmically managed portfolios, low-cost ETFs, and AI-powered analytics to investors of all sizes.

History & Growth

Founded by ex-Wall Street analysts, our platform launched with a flagship robo-advisor in early 2019. By 2021 we had crossed $1 billion in assets under management and expanded into taxable accounts, IRAs, and crypto-allocation strategies.

Mission & Philosophy

Our mission is to democratize access to professional investment strategies. We adhere to evidence-based, academically vetted models:

  • Mean-variance optimization for diversified portfolios.
  • Factor-based tilts towards value, momentum, and quality.
  • Continuous rebalancing to maintain target allocations.

Core Values

  • Transparency — clear fee breakdowns and performance reporting.
  • Accessibility — intuitive UI, mobile apps, and tiered account levels.
  • Innovation — ongoing research collaborations with top universities.

Leadership Team

  • Alice Johnson, CEO — 20 years in asset management and fintech entrepreneurship.
  • Robert Lee, CIO — PhD in Financial Economics, ex-quant at HedgeFundX.
  • Maria Gonzalez, Head of Research — leads a team of data scientists and analysts.

Roadmap & Vision

Upcoming features include:

  • Goals-based planning tools with scenario simulations.
  • Fractional share trading across global markets.
  • Social investing community features and copy-trading.

Achievements

  • “Robo-Advisor of the Year” by FinanceTech Awards 2022.
  • Secured $75 M in Series C funding led by Growth Partners.
  • 100 000+ active investors with average portfolio growth of 12 % annually.
